[**26POOL**](http://world-pool.edselserrano.com/) is a global prediction pool for the 2026 World Cup (USA · Canada · Mexico): one single pool, no fees and no entry, where you call the exact score of every match before kickoff and your ranking shifts the moment the final whistle blows.

[![26POOL screenshot — the 2026 World Cup landing screen](/world-pool.webp)](http://world-pool.edselserrano.com/)

## What 26POOL is

The pitch is simple: *Call the score. Climb the table.* Before each match you submit an exact-score pick. No groups to set up, no fees, no invites — everyone competes in the same global pool. When a match ends and the final score is entered, points are awarded instantly and the leaderboard re-sorts live.

## How scoring works

The points system rewards precision:

- **5 points** — exact score (you nailed the full result).
- **3 points** — correct winner + goal difference.
- **1 point** — correct winner only.

This scale keeps the table alive until the very last match: a single exact score can flip the standings in one matchday.

## The kickoff lock

My favorite design detail: **picks lock at kickoff at the database level**. It isn't a check that lives only in the frontend — the database itself rejects late writes. You can edit your pick as many times as you like until the match starts; after that, the door closes for everyone equally. Zero advantage for anyone who refreshes a second too late.

## Tech stack

26POOL is built with **Next.js** and **React**, deployed on **Vercel**. The interface is clean and minimal, with bilingual support (EN/ES) and light/dark mode. The demo card on the landing page shows a sample knockout bracket — quarters, semis, and final — with your pick, the points earned, and your daily rank; real data appears once matches begin.
